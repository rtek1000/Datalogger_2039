- Keyboard:
- - Insert the keyboard into the primary USB (FS) port
- - The "Num Lock" indicator should be turned on automatically
- - Quickly press the ESC key several times to reset

- Button
- - Quick pulse to change sleep state (Helps to recognize USB keyboard)
- - Hold for more than 15 seconds to reset

- PrintScreen:
- - Insert the USB flash drive device into the secondary port (HS)
- - Keyboard shortcut for screenshot is "Ctrl + Prt Sc"
   (Control + Print Screen)
